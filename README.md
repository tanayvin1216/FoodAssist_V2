## 📖 The Story

When I learned about the **Carteret County Food & Health Council's** mission to connect residents with food assistance resources, I saw an opportunity to make a real difference. The council was managing their directory manually—Google Forms feeding into spreadsheets, Word documents for reports, and countless hours of volunteer work just to keep information up to date. This project aims to help the **10,240** people who face hunger in Carteret County.

**I built this application to serve the Carteret County community** by creating a modern, accessible platform that helps:

- **Families** find food pantries and hot meal programs near them
- **Volunteers** discover opportunities to give back to their community  
- **Donors** see exactly what's needed and where
- **Food assistance organizations** keep their information current without technical barriers
- **The Food & Health Council** coordinate resources across the county efficiently

---

## Why This Matters

**Carteret County** is home to beautiful coastal communities—Beaufort, Morehead City, Atlantic Beach, and many more. But like communities everywhere, residents sometimes need help accessing food resources.

### The Challenge

Before this platform, finding food assistance meant:

- ❌ Calling multiple organizations hoping they're open
- ❌ Driving to locations only to find different hours
- ❌ Not knowing which pantries serve your area
- ❌ Missing out on available help because information was outdated

### The Solution

This directory provides:

- ✅ **Real-time information** on food pantries, hot meal programs, and delivery services
- ✅ **Search by ZIP code** to find help in your neighborhood  
- ✅ **See hours, contact info, and services** all in one place
- ✅ **Mobile-friendly** so you can search on the go
- ✅ **Always up-to-date** because organizations can update their own listings

## Key Features

### 🔍 Smart Search & Filtering
Find exactly what you need by searching for organizations by name, filtering by town, ZIP code, type of assistance, or even which days they're open.

### 📱 Mobile-First Design
Whether you're using a phone, tablet, or computer, the directory works beautifully. Large buttons, clear text, and simple navigation designed with accessibility in mind.

### 🕐 Real-Time Updates
Organizations can log in and update their hours, services, and contact information instantly. No more calling to check if they're open—the info you see is current.

### 🤝 Volunteer Connection
Organizations post their volunteer needs, and community members can respond. It's that simple.

### 📊 Admin Dashboard
The Food & Health Council can oversee all listings, track donations to pantries, and generate reports—all from one central hub.

### 🌐 Spanish Support Ready
Built with bilingual support in mind to serve Carteret County's diverse community.
